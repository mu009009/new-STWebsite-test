Signals
A powerful augmented intelligence engine for your business.
Simple. Powerful. Fast.
Signals is a comprehensive analytics platform transforming latent data into unified business insight across Social, 3rd Party and Enterprise systems, at the speed of business.
We Start with Data
In addition to our growing list of pre-built data connectors for everything from Hadoop to Facebook, Signals also processes ad hoc and streaming data from internal data sources and 3rd party systems like SalesForce. No matter what the source, getting data into Signals is secure, fast and easy.
We Use Machine Learning
Signals “learns” the data using patented analytical processing techniques and performs enhanced analysis for Sentiment, geography, temporality and a host of other structured data dimensions that help unify analysis across multiple data collections.
We Tell the Data Story
Intuitive visual elements make finding stories and insights hidden within terabytes of data quick and easy. Navigating from large quantities of comments down to just a few, identifying root cause, or illuminating specific points of interest is simple and fast.
We Enable Collaboration
Sharing insights across the organization drives action. Signals allows people to Snapshot, export and share visualizations with everyone, even if they aren’t a Signals user, ensuring that important fact-based information doesn’t stay hidden.
